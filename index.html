Most B2B SaaS companies track competitor pricing manually, checking competitor websites weekly and updating spreadsheets. This approach misses 60% of pricing changes and consumes 3-5 hours weekly per competitor.

1. Automated Daily Monitoring

  • Use tools like Tradie Radar to automatically screenshot competitor pricing pages

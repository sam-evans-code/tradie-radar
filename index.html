Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tradie Radar

Will the caller know they're not speaking to me?

Not unless you want them to. Our team is trained to answer as your business. We learn your business name, service area, typical jobs, and pricing structure. To the caller, it sounds like they've reached your office — not a generic answering service.

We're not reading from a script. We're having a real conversation to understand what they need, when they need it, and whether it's the kind of job you want to hear about immediately.

How does the call forwarding work?

It's called "conditional call forwarding" — your phone forwards to us only if you don't pick up within 2 rings. If you answer, we never see the call. If you're busy, it forwards to us automatically.

Setup takes 2 minutes. You dial a code (we'll send it to you), enter our number, done. You can turn it on and off whenever you like. No app. No new number. No changing your business cards.

What counts as a "golden goose" job?

We work with you to define it, but typically it's one of these:

  • Commercial work — facilities managers, property management companies, businesses
  • Multi-property landlords — someone who owns 5+ rental units and needs a reliable tradesperson
  • Emergency callouts — "I need someone now" jobs that pay premium rates
  • High-value projects — jobs worth £1,000+ that you'd want to know about immediately

Everything else (small repairs, tire-kickers, price shoppers) gets handled politely, booked in your calendar if appropriate, but doesn't interrupt your workday.

What happens to calls that aren't golden goose?

We handle them politely and professionally. Depending on what you prefer, we can:

  • Book them into your calendar for a callback later
  • Take full details and send you a text summary at end of day
  • Give them a rough quote if it's standard work
  • Politely decline if it's outside your service area or expertise

The goal is simple: you only get interrupted for calls that are worth interrupting you for. Everything else gets handled without pulling you off the tools.

What's the free missed call audit?

We'll ask you to estimate how many calls you miss per week, what percentage might be high-value jobs, and your average job value. Then we'll show you roughly what those missed calls could be costing you per month.

Most tradespeople are shocked when they see the number. It's not a sales pitch — it's just math. If it makes sense to fix the problem, we'll talk about how Tradie Radar works. If not, no hard feelings. At least you'll know what you're leaving on the table.
